Admission

Selection

The St. Thomas School runs from April to March and admissions are normally completed between January and April for most classes.

Needless to say, there is a long waiting list and it is best to get in touch with the school well in advance of any proposed move.There are entrance assessments and interviews for entry into the middle and senior schools.

Students should be of good academic ability and of good character.The school may ask for references, since living together at Quality of the School is really as important as following its academic programs.

If the child come from another state T.C. has to be counter signed by the education officer of that place for the students seeking admission from abroad T.C. has to be countersigned by the embassy of the country from where pupil is seeking admission.

The name and DOB once entered cannot be altered without the written permission of the competent authority.

Satisfactory testimonials of good conduct is required for pupil who come from other institution.

Admission Criteria

Admission for every academic year starts from the first week of November.

Parents seeking admission to any class for their ward are required to apply in a prescribed form available at the office.

Admission to Nursery, LKG, UKG and Class I & II will be through an interview and on first come first serve basis according to the prescribed age requirements.

Admission for Class III to VIII will be on the basis of an entrance test and an interview. Subject for admission test for Class III to Vill are English, Mathematics & Hindi

The entrance test papers will be based on syllabus of the preceding class to which the admission is sought The written test will be followed by a personal interview of the student

Date of admission test/interview will be intimated by the school office at the time of Registration.

Students from the State Board, ICSE and any statutory Board outside Uttar Pradesh are eligible to take admission in School.

Beyond The Classroom
To provide excellent learning atmosphere where the potential and talents of each child are developed to their maximum which will enable them to attain self actualisation and thereby lead the development of society as responsible citizens.

School Timing
Class timing are from 09:00 am to 02:00 pm.

Office timing 08:30 am to 03:30 pm.

Parents can meet the teachers only after 2 pm. Parents are not allowed to meet the teachers when school is in the session.

Visitors including previous students can meet teachers only at staff room after class timing. Parents can meet principal at 01:00 pm to 03:30 pm.

Examination
Class tests are held every month. These test are very important.

Parent can keep close watch on the progress of their wards through these marks.

At St. Thomas School, teaching isn't just about testing the memory of students.

A lot of attention is paid by our teachers at an individual level for each child. Promotion will be granted on the basis of marks obtained in these examinations.

Educational Tours & Excursion
Picnic, excursion and visits to various places of historical interest and other important places from the salient features of the intuition. Besides this wherever possible arrangement are also made for educational tours to different parts of the country during mid term breaks.

Uniforms
Sample materials or pattern for uniform will be provided from the office. Parents can purchase the same material from any suitable shop but design & pattern should be strictly followed.

School Fee

All fees should be paid at the school office in the morning from 09:00 am to 03:00 pm.

Fee can also bepaid online by using NEFT by the bank given on the school website.

If fee are not paid by the due date, the student may be debarred from sitting in the class and if dues exceed two quarter fees, their names may be strike off from the register and to continue, re-admission fee shall be charged.
